2. A.If the verb ends with vowel add what?B.If the verb ends with consonant add what?​


A. If the verb ends with vowel:

• "e", add only "d." For example, arrive + d = arrived

• "y," add "-ed". For example, play + ed = played

B. If the verb ends in consonant and a "y," change the "y" to "i" and add "-ed". For example, "study + ed = studied".

6. The ends of a tuning fork oscillate at a frequency of 440 Hz with an amplitude of 0.50 mm. Determine (a) the maximum velocity and (b) the maximum acceleration of the ends.​


(a) The maximum velocity of the ends can be determined using the equation:

v_max = A * ω

where A is the amplitude and ω is the angular frequency given by

ω = 2πf

Substituting the given values, we have:

v_max = 0.5 mm * (2π * 440 Hz) = 2828 mm/s

(b) The maximum acceleration of the ends can be determined using the equation:

a_max = ω^2 * A

Substituting the given values, we have:

a_max = (2π * 440 Hz)^2 * 0.5 mm = 3.12 x 10^7 mm/s^2

Note: The units for velocity and acceleration should be consistent, in this case mm/s and mm/s^2 respectively

26. I have a jail. I have rules. With me you can travel the world


I have a jail. I have rules. With me you can travel the world. The correct answer to this riddle is: airplane.

Artinya adalah "aku punya penjara, aku punya aturan. Denganku kamu bisa keliling dunia. Jawaban yang tepat bagi riddle di atas adalah airplane (pesawat)".

Pembahasan

Kalimat di atas mudah kita pahami karena disusun dari simple present tense, sebuah tenses yang paling sederhana dan dasar dalam bahasa Inggris. Kita lihat tiga kalimat di atas memberikan kita "clue", yaitu apa yang mempunyai rules (aturan), seperti jail (penjara) yang kita tidak bisa bebas keluar masuk, dan bisa keliling dunia. Tentu saja "clue" yang dimaksud adalah pesawat (airplane).
