5. She will call you Tolong di batu ke passive voice


Bentuk passive voice dari kalimat "she will call you" adalah...

Karena setelah subject "she" menggunakan will+V1 "will call" maka kalimat di atas termasuk ke dalam SIMPLE FUTURE TENSE. Dan rumus passive nya adalah sbb :
Subject + will + be + V3 + by...

Ingat !!!
Subject di kalimat aktif berubah jadi object di kalimat pasif, begitu pula sebaliknya.

Jadi, jawabannya adalah..
Active : She will call you.
Passive : You WILL BE CALLED by her.

7. buat kalimat active voice dan passive voice, dari kata : 1. boil - boiled 2. call - called 3. use - used 4. paint - painted ​


Jawaban:

1. My mother boils the water - The water is boiled by my mother.

2. I call my friend - My friend is called by me.

3. My brother uses my shoes - My shoes are used by my brother.

4. Budi paints my house - My house is painted by Budi

Penjelasan:

Dalam Simple Present Tense, rumus kalimat pasif adalah O + To be (is, are) + V3 + by + Subject.

Contoh:

The book is bought by my friend.

The book : Object

is : to be

bought : V3

by

My friend : Subject

Apabila di ubah menjadi kalimat aktif:

My friend buys the book.

19. passive voice dar kalimat we often call the rain's phenomenon as " water circle"​


The rain's phenomenon is often called as "water circle"

Semoga membantu, maaf kalau salah

Karena ada “often” maka kalimat ini bentuk Present Tense:

(+) S + V1 (s/es) + O/C

Active: we often call the rain's phenomenon as "water circle”

Maka Jawabannya:

S + To Be (is/am/are) + V3 + by (agent)

Passive: The rain’s phenomenon is often called [by us] as water circle

Catatan:

=> Perubahan dari Active ke Passive yaitu dengan menukar posisi antara Subject (We) dan Object (the rain’s phenomenon) lalu gunakan To Be + V3.

* V1 call = V3 called
