29. bantuin bahasa inggris dongChoose 5 sentences from the passage below and change it into passive voice. Today most cars use gasoline, but in the future many people may drive electric cars. Electric cars do not pollute the air. Electricity from a battery powers the motor of an electric car. Drivers of electric cars do not fill their cars with gasoline, they connect their cars to an electrical outlet to charge the battery with electricity. The driver of an electric car connects the car to an electrical outlet at night. In the morning, the battery is charge with enough electricity to drive all day. Electric cars are not as fast as gasoline powered cars, and they cannot travel more than 150 miles (270 km). After 150 miles, the driver must charge the battery again. However, electric cars may be one answer to the problems of pollution and high gasoline prices.​


Jawaban:

1. Electric cars do not pollute the air.

The air doesn't get polluted by electric cars.

2. Electricity from a battery powers the motor of an electric car.

The motor of an electric car is powered by electricity from a battery.

3. Drivers of electric cars do not fill their cars with gasoline, they connect their cars to an electrical outlet to charge the battery with electricity.

Gasoline is not filled to the car by the drivers, the cars are connected to an electrical outlet by them to charge the battery with electricity.

4. The driver of an electric car connects the car to an electrical outlet at night.

An electric car is connected to an electrical outlet at night by the driver.

5. After 150 miles, the driver must charge the battery again.

The battery must be changed by the driver again after 150 miles.

Penjelasan:

Struktur passive voice secara umum:

Subject + to be (am/is/are) + kata kerja jenis v3

Penting : Objek yang ada dalam kalimat aktif diubah menjadi subjek di kalimat pasif.
