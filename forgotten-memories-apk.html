1. Yang benar itu “thank you all for the memories” atau “thank you for all memories” atau “thank you for all the memories” ? Mohon bantuannya


Penullsan yang benar adalah "Thank you for all the memories"

Pembahasan

There are different meanings of memory.

The faculty by which the mind stores and remembers

I have a great memory about his face.

Something remembered from the past.

I don't have any good memories of my childhood.

So, from the questions, we assume that you are grateful for having good memories with someone that you remember until now.

You could say "Thank you for all the memories" which has meaning terima kasih atas semua kenangan itu.
