PEMBAHASANSimple Present Tense

Yaitu bentuk tenses yang digunakan untuk menunjukkan suatu kejadian yang sedang berlangsung atau berulangkali.

RUMUS

Positive Sentences:

Subject + Verb 1 + ObjectSubject + to be + Object

Negative Sentences:

Subject + do/does + not + Verb 1 + ObjectSubject + to be + not + Object

Interrogative Sentences:

Do/does + Subject + Verb 1 + Object + ?To be + Subject + Object + ?

Based on Question

~ My cats (to be) very cute.

Setelah subjek terdapat to be yang kurang. Setelah to be ada sifat (adjective). Maka kalimat tersebut berupa Present Simple.

My cats (to be) very cute.

➡ My cats are very cute.

14. ubah ke kalimat tanya my younger brother is very cute​


Jawaban:

(?) is my younger brother very cute?

PEMBAHASAN Present Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan Kegiatan yg terjadi secara berulang, kebiasaan, rutin dan hal2 bersifat umum.

Rumus Present Tense

1. Verbal Sentence

(+) S + v1 (-s/es)(-) S + do/does + not + V1 (?) Do/does + S + v1?

2. Nominal Sentence

(+) S + is/am/are + Complement (-) S + is/am/are + not + Complement (?) is/am/are + s + complement?

Yang ditanyakan adalah Ubah ke kalimat tanya my younger brother is very cute

Berikut Berdasarkan Rumus Nominal Sentence:

(+) S + is/am/are + Complement

➡ my younger brother is very cute

(-) S + is/am/are + not + Complement

➡ my younger brother is not very cute

(?) is/am/are + s + complement?

➡ is my younger brother very cute?
