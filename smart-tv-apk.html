24. Bagaimana cara nya mengunduh saluran digital,samsung smart tv? Jawabb yaa


For Automatic Tuning:: Step 1.    Go to MENU, and select Channel.Step 2.    On the Channel menu, select Auto Tuning.Step 3.    The TV will now automatically scan for available channels. For Manual Tuning: Manual tuning can be used when your TV has been unsuccessful at locking all known or available channels in your broadcast area. It can be used on both Digital and Analogue tuning modes. Step 1:  Select Menu > Channel > Manual Tuning.Step 2:  Select either Digital Channel Tuning or Analogue Channel Tuning depending on the channel type you want to add.Step 3: The list of currently tuned channels will appear.Step 4: Select New to tune new channel.Step 5: New Channel window will appear.Step 6: Select whether to enter Channel or Frequency.Step 7: After inputting desired Channel or Frequency, select Search and press Enter.Step 8: The TV will search for channels.Step 9: If there is sufficient signal the TV will memorise the channels at that frequency.Step 10: If there is no signal at that frequency, the Signal Quality indicator will be 0% (nothing showing). If there is low signal (10 ~ 30%), the TV might still lock some of the channels, but the picture will pause/freeze/break-up, and likely “popping” noise in the audio. Both of these situations would indicate an issue with the reception and not the TV itself. (di translate pke google translate klo tidak mengerti)
