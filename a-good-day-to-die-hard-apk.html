In order to read a lots of interesting novels 2 She studies hard. She wants to have a good job in the future. She studies hard. So that have a good job in the future. I worked hard. Write these sentences using in order to or so that

I study italian so I can read a lots of interesting novels. She studies hard, in order to have a good job in the future. I worked hard so that I could pay the debt. My cousin Peter works day by day in order to have a better life. I will travel to Korea so that I can meet my pen friend. I buy a lot of chips so I will eat them tonight. Sarah studied hard so that she could pass the test. Pembahasan

Penggunaan so that dan in order to sama-sama sebagai conjunction atau kata penghubung. Keduanya menghubungkan kalimat aksi dan tujuannya. Perbedaannya adalah so that digunakan untuk sehari-hari atau umum. Sedangkan in order to biasanya digunakan untuk kalimat formal atau situasi formal seperti dalam penulisan jurnal.

Rumus yang perlu diperhatikan adalah

an action clause + so that + subject + a purpose clause. an action clause + in order to + infinitive + a purpose clause.

Contoh dalam kalimat adalah

He studies hard in order to pass the exam.

She studied hard so that she could pass the test.
