Subject + is/am/are + Verb 3 + by + Object.

2. Kalimat pasiv all plants need fertilizer​


Soal bentuk Present Tense

(+) S + V1 (s/es) + O

Active: all plants need fertilizer​

Maka Rumus passive:

S + To be (is/am/are) + V3 + by (agent)

Passive: Fertilizer is needed by All plants

Catatan:

Perubahan Passive yaitu dengan cara menukar posisi antara Subjek and Objek lalu gunakan To be + Verb 3

V1 need = V3 needed

============================

Jawaban:

The farmer can plant 3 chilli plants per 0.5 m². To find out how many plants he can plant in 85 m², you can divide the total area by the area per plant.

85 m² / 0.5 m²/plant = 170 plants.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

The area per plant is given as 0.5 m², which means that one chilli plant takes up 0.5 square meters of space. To find out how many plants can be planted in a given area, we need to divide that area by the area per plant. In this case, the given area is 85 m². So, we can calculate the number of chilli plants that can be planted in 85 m² by dividing 85 by 0.5.

85 m² / 0.5 m²/plant = 170 plants.

This means that in an area of 85 m², the farmer can plant 170 chilli plants.
