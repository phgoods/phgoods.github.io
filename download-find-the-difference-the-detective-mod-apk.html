30. The first term of an arithmetic sequence is 14. The fourth term is 32. Find the common difference.


Answer:

The n-th term of an arithmetic sequence is given by:

Un = a + (n - 1)b

Where a the first term, b the common difference. If U4 = 32 and a = 14 then

32 = 14 + (4 - 1)b

18 = 3b

b = 6

The common difference is 6
