Past tense:

(+) S + V2 + O

(-) S + did not + V1 + O

(?) did + s + v1 + o

Maka:

1.(+) We went to Bandung last month

(-) we did not go to Bandung last month

(?) did we go to Bandung last month

(-) Katie didn't work three last year

(?) did Katie work three last year
