9. Active voice : The students buy the bags. Passive voice : _______________________


Answer:

passive voice:

The bags are bought by the students.

Discussion:

Diperintahkan untuk merubah kalimat aktif pada soal ke dalam bentuk pasif. Agar dapat merubahnya, amati terlebih dahulu kata-kata yang mendukung kalimatnya.

Kalimat pasif atau disebut passive voice merupakan bentuk kalimat yang menyatakan obyek berperan sebagai subyek dimana obyeknya diperankan oleh subyek. Pada kalimat pasif terdapat perubahan dalam verbanya. Umumnya menggunakan past participle (V3).

Supaya bisa merubahnya, mari kita ketahui terlebih dahulu:

kalimat terdapat perubahan verbamemerhatikan to be apa yang digunakan berdasarkan tense-nyaobyek mendahului subyek (obyek + to be/aux + verba 3 + subyek)

Karena kalimat yang diberikan pada soal berbentuk simple present dengan menyatakan verba dalam bentuk kesatu dan keterangan waktu tidak diketahui yang dianggap terjadi saat kini (in the present).

S + V1(-s/es) + O + ...

O + to be (is/am/are) + V3 + by — S + ...dengan to be diperhatikan dari obyeknya (is: he, she, it, name, object | am: I | are: you, they, we, names, objects)verba dari simple present diubah menjadi bentuk verba untuk pasif (past participle (V3))titik-titik dapat diisi sebagai sisa kalimat misalkan keterangannya (C)

Explanation:

Kita dapat perhatikan bentuk kata masing-masing:

the students: subject (S)buy: verb-infinitive (V1)the bags: object (O)

Maka dapat diubah menjadi:

The bags (O) + are (to be) + bought (V3) + by the students (S)

dengan to be dipilih is karena subyeknya orang ketiga jamak

Kalimat pasifnya seperti ini:

The bags are bought by the students.

19. Active voice : The students buy the bags. Passive voice : _______________________


Answer:

Passive voice: The bags are bought by the students.

___^^____

Explanation:

Now these are the ways how to know transforming active voice into passive, so let's learn it.

—> generally used past-participle verb (V3)

—> observe the to be and what tense is used

—> prior the object and transform the subject into the agent

We know this pattern.

O + to be (is/am/are) + V3 + by agent

-> agent also known as the subject

-> don't forget to use the proper to be according the object

  —> is: he, she, it, singular object/person

  —> am: I

  —> are: you, they, we, plural object/person

Now let's find the structure of the sentence:

The students (S) buy (V1) the bags (O)

—> the sentence is in simple present

So here's the passive voice:

The bags are bought by students.

25. Active Voice: The man plays several instruments in the concert. Passive Voice: ​


Several instruments in the concerts are played by the man.

EXPLANATION

•» Passive Voice yaitu dimana subject kalimat (yang biasanya melakukan aksi jika pada active voice) menjadi penerima aksi.

RUMUS

Passive Voice - Simple Present Tense

Subject + to be (is/am/are) + V3 + Object.

•» Active Voice Simple Present

Subject + verb 1 + object

The man plays several instruments in the concert.

=> subjek di sini merupakan nama orang karena melakukan aksi (dalam active voice)

•» Passive Voice Simple Present

Subject + to be (is/am/are) + V3 + Object.

Several instruments in the concerts are played by the man.

=> subjek di sini merupakan kata benda (noun), nama orang pada passive voice menjadi penerima aksi.

Rumus Passive Voice yang lain di antaranya:

Simple Past : Subject + was/were + verb 3 + object.

Simple Future : S + will be + verb 3 + object.
