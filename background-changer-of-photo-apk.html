17. Apkah definisi dari background of the study dan statement of the problem ?.


Answer:

In doing or making research on all kinds of field of studies, including English, those two parts are inseparable.
Background of study is the information on stuff which leads you to choose that main focus on your research. So basically it explains why you choose such focus and title of your final project or thesis. Example:
I did a research back then on the depiction of hedonism in F Scott Fitzgerald short stories. So the background of study is telling the reason why you choose those short stories, F Scott Fitzgerald' work and hedonism itself, ideally relate them to what happens today.
Statement of problems is the statement of your research focus. Usually you need to have two or three statement of problems, which later needs analysing and proving. In the example of mine, the statements I used were
- hedonism difference in both stories
- how the ideas of hedonism depicted in both stories.

Kelas: SMA
Mapel: English
Kategori : research

Background of study, is about the research on which you base your study problem, why are you doing it. A review paper gives an overview of research dealing with a specific topic. For instance, in our blog.
The background of a study is the information that a colleague who is in your field would need to know if they want to understand your research. It would give technical information and it would show that there are somethings that are not known.

A statement problem is one where certain conditions are stated, in a series of statements. The person solving the problem may have to do algebra equations, in order to solve the problem. There are also statements which must be solved logically. For example: 1) A man walks out of his front door, and walks a mile South. 2) He then walks a mile West, and shoots a bear. 3) He then walks a mile North, to his home. Question: What color is the bear?
