5. pengertian fairytale, folktale, myth, legend, dan fable


Kelas : SMA 
Mapel : Bhs. Inggris
Kategori : Meaning
Kata Kunci : fantasy

Semua kata yang yang akan diartikan termasuk kata yang muncul dalam kategori bahasa yang muncul di cerita fiksi. 

Fairytale : adalah genre dimana cerita yang dibawakan adalah fantasi atau tidak nyata, karakter dalam cerita tersebut hanya karangan dan biasanya lokasi cerita tersebut berada di dunia lain atau area yang tidak nyata. Contoh fairytale adalah Harry Potter, Angling Dharma dan Cincerella

Folktale : adalah cerita berdasarkan cerita cerita masa lalu orang jaman dahulu. keaslian cerita ini sangat diragukan karena folktale diceritakan mulut kemulut tanpa adanya transkrip atau tulisan tertulis. Contoh Folktale Malin Kumbang

Myth : kurang lebih sama seperti folktale namun cerita dari myth/mitos terkadang dapat ditemukan di kehidupan moderen. Biasanya mitos menceritakan tentang makhluk makhluk yang belum diketahui keasliannya. Contoh Myth adalah Bigfoot, Yeti

Legend : adalah cerita kombinasi dari folktale dan fairytale, disatu sisi legend adalah cerita rakyat yang dipadukan dengan elemen fantasi sehingga selain terdengar nyata namun juga ada susasana heroik dalam cerita tersebut. Contoh legend : legenda viking dari norway beserta dewa2 mereka.

Fable : adalah cerita fantasi dimana karakternya adalah hewan. Fable utamanya menceritakan cerita yang ditujukan ke manusia namun melalui karakter binatang yang dapat berbicara dan menggunakan pergerakan dan watak manusia. Contoh Fable si Kancil

1) fairy tale is a simple children's story that usually involves imaginary creatures and magic such as fairies, wizards, and goblins

2) folk tale is a tale or legend originating and traditional that usually with characteristically anonymous, timeless, and placeless which circulated orally among a people.

3) myth is a popular belief or traditional story of ostensibly historical events, especially explaining the early history of a group of people or about natural events and facts.

4) legend is a very old story or set of stories from ancient times and popularly accepted as historical that tells about a famous event or person, but not always true

5) fable is a simple children's story that usually involves animals as characters, conveying a moral.

19. 5 pertanyaan serta jawaban tentang myth dalam bahasa inggris


1. Where the story of "Beowulf" was occurred?
The people of Beowulf's kingdom identified themselves as the Geat, which is what the Modern Sweden acknowledged as their medieval ancestors.

2. What is the reason of the Trojan War happened? 
Paris, the prince of Troy, was stealing Helen, the wife of Menelaus the brother of Agemmemnon. Paris was prophesied that he would destroy his kingdom as Hera, Aphrodite, and Athena fought over his preference. Using the abduction as a reason to attack Troy, the Greeks assembled their army while the Greek Gods are using the war as a contest of which their blessed warriors would gain glory.

3. How King Arthur died?
King Arthur, who clashed with one of his knight Lancelot, went to war against the Frankish after Lancelot abducted Arthur's wife Guinevere from her execution because of her and Lancelot's affair. Another Knight of the Round Table, Mordred, who is unknowingly Arthur's bastard son, roused a rebellion when Arthur was away. Hearing about the revolt, Arthur quickly returned to Camelot and fought Mordred in the hill of Camlann. Both Arthur and Mordred died striking each other.

4. Why Arjuna won Draupadi's engagement challenge despite Karna also completing the challenge?
Karna, who actually the Pandava's older brother, was raised as a charioteer son and everyone was ignorant of his heritage. By the law of Hindu caste, royalty can't marry those who came from lower castes, only can marrying from the royalty and the priest caste. Arjuna and brothers, who were disguising themselves as priests, had the right to be married to the royalty caste.

5. Why Brynhildr didn't marry Sigurd despite they were loving each other?   
Sigurd drunk a mind-wiping wine from Grimhild so he could marry her daughter Gudrun. Sigurd, who forgot about his promise with Brynhildr, made to promise Gudrun that he would bring Brynhildr to the wife of Gudrun's brother Gunnar. Thus, Sigurd was married to Gudrun, while Brynhildr was married to Gunnar.
