23. we ate apple and banana for our breakfast last morning buat kn kalimat pasif​


Jawaban:

Apple and banana were eaten by us for breakfast last morning

Pembahasan

We ate apple and banana for our breakfast last morning

Structure Kalimatnya:Subject => WeVerb 2 => ateObject => apple and banana Complement => for our breakfast last morning

Berdasarkan keterangan di atas:Kalimat bentuk aktif, karena Subjek melakukan aksi dalam kalimatnya Kalimat bentuk Past Tense karena ada keterangan waktu lampau Last Morning

Berdasarkan Rumus Past Tense

Active Voice: Kalimat aktif, Subjek melakukan aksi/tindakan dalam kalimatnya.

(+) S + V2 + O

=> We ate apple and banana for our breakfast last morning

Passive Voice: Kalimat pasif, Objek dikenai aksi/tindakan oleh subjek dalam kalimatnya.

(+) O + Be (was/were) + V3 + By S

=> Apple and banana were eaten by us for breakfast last morning

Catatan:

Menggunakan Tobe WERE karena Subjek jamak yaitu apple and banana

6y = 6 x 0,45
