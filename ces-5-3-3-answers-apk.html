5. PCS 3 senten ces b. Inggris.jangan pada ngasal mudah² yang ngasal masuk neraka aminnnn​


pcs 3 sentence ces b. inggris (maksudnya present continuous, buat 3 contoh kalimat kah?)

rumus :

(+) subjek + to be (am,is,are) + verb *ing + objek

(-) s + to be + not + v ing + o

(?) to be + s + v ing + o?

example 3 sentences:

- children are going to school

- the boys are playing in the park

- the baby is crying in the kitchen

kalau pakai rumus rumus nya :

1. (+) children are going to school

(-) children are not going to school

(?) are children going to school?

2. (+) the boys are playing in the park

(-) the boys are not playing in the park

(?) are the boys playing in the park?

3. (+) the baby is crying in the kitchen

(-) the baby is not crying in the kitchen

(?) is the baby crying in the kitchen?
