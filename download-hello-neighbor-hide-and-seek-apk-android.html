30. are-?-children-the-hide and seek-playing​


Jawaban:

Are the children playing hide and seek?

PEMBAHASAN

Present Continuous Tense adalah tenses yg digunakan utk menyatakan di masa sekarang (present) yg sedang berlangsung (continous).

RUMUS:

(+) S + To Be (is/am/are) + V-ing + O

(-) S + To Be (is/am/are) + not + V-ing + O

(?) To Be (is/am/are) + S + V-ing + O?

=> Subject + To Be:I + amyou, we, they, + areHe, she, it + is

=> Keterangan Waktu: Now right nowat the momentat the presenttoday

KESIMPULANKalimat bentuk tanya karena ada tanda tanya (?)Ada Subject (the children), To Be (are) dan Verb-ing (playing)Maka gunakan Rumus Present Continuous

(?) To Be (is/am/are) + S + V-ing + O?

= Are + the children + playing + hide and seek?

(Apakaha anak-anak sedang bermain petak-umpet?)
