1) Attach the strap / seatbelt
2) Turn the key to start the engine (On position)
3) Turn on the AC
4) Lower Brake Hand (Brake hand free)
5) Hit the clutch runs out, move the gear to gear 1
6) Remove the clutch slowly
7) Step on the gas while little by little until you feel the car moving. Attention: you must do it slowly, so that you don’t get accident.

1) Hit the clutch runs
2) Lift gas
3) Move the gear teeth to a desired

21. If l had locked the car. The thief wouldn't have stolen my car. Buat real fact nya​


Jawaban:

the fact is the thief had stolen my car.

Penjelasan:

semoga jelas

CONDITIONAL SENTENCE

If l had locked the car. The thief wouldn't have stolen my car.

Fact ➡ I didn't lock the car, so the thief stole my car.

EXPLANATION

Conditional sentence adalah kalimat andai², di mana suatu keinginan dapat terwujud apabila persyaratannya terpenuhi. Conditional sentence tersusun atas if clause dan main clause/result clause. If clause menggambarkan kondisinya, dan main clause menggambarkan konsekuensinya.

Conditional tipe 1 [First conditional] menjabarkan keinginan yang kemungkinan terjadi saat ini ataupun nanti-nanti (real possibility) berdasarkan prediksi.

Rumus :

If+Simple present, Simple future

If+S+verb1(s/es)..., S+will+verb1......

Exp.

If she finishes her study, she will go abroad.

Fact ➡ She probably goes abroad

Conditional tipe 2 [Second conditional] menjabarkan keinginan yang diragukan terjadi saat ini atau mendatang.

Rumus :

If+Simple past, Past future

If+S+verb2 ......, S+would+verb1......

Exp.

If I came to your house, your sister wouldn't be sad.

Fact ➡ Your sister is sad because I don't come to your house.

Conditional tipe 3 [The third conditional] menjabarkan keinginan yang tidak terwujud di waktu lampau. Pengulasannya saat ini hanyalah sebagai dampak dari kejadian di waktu lampau.

Rumus :

If+Past perfect, Past future perfect

If+S+had+verb3...., S+would+have+verb3....

Exp.

If he had told the truth, his boss wouldn't have been angry.

Fact ➡ he didn't tell the truth, so his boss was angry.
