= Do you want to play?

2.to-my- brother-play-mask-likes

= my brother likes to play mask

3.slide-play-randi-to-wants

= Randi wants to play slide

Catatan:

Ketiga soal kalimat acak tersebut merupakan bentuk Present Tense yg memiliki pola/rumus:

(+) S + V1 (s/es) + O

(-) S + does/do + not + V1 + O

(?) do/does + S + V1 + O?
